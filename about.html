Our Journey

2023
The Problem
Witnessing enterprises struggle with unprotected AI adoption, our founder recognized a critical gap: organizations needed real-time LLM data protection they could trust and control completely, without per-call fees or vendor lock-in.
Early 2024
Building the Shield
We assembled a team of security and reactive systems engineers to build a proxy architecture capable of filtering sensitive data at unprecedented speed. The goal: protect AI interactions without impacting user experience.
Mid 2024
Performance Breakthrough
Our pattern detection engine achieved sub-50ms P95 latency with 95% accuracy. Combined with bidirectional filtering and reversible tokenization, Data Hawk became the fastest self-hosted LLM security solution available.
Today
Securing Enterprise AI
Data Hawk now protects LLM interactions for organizations worldwide, filtering sensitive data in real-time across user inputs, AI outputs, training data, and developer tools. Our commitment to self-hosted excellence continues to drive innovation.
