Securing the World's Most Sensitive Data

Born from the realization that enterprises need complete control over their data governance, Data Hawk empowers organizations to discover, classify, and protect sensitive information without compromising sovereignty or breaking the bank.

Our Mission

In an era where data breaches cost millions and compliance violations threaten business continuity, we believe every organization deserves enterprise-grade data protection that they fully control.

Data Hawk was founded on a simple principle: Your data should never leave your infrastructure. We're building the future of self-hosted data governance, where security meets sovereignty.

Our reactive architecture processes petabytes at lightning speed, while our commitment to open standards ensures you're never locked into a vendor ecosystem.

Our Journey

2023
The Spark
After witnessing enterprises struggle with expensive, vendor-locked data governance solutions, our founders recognized a critical gap: organizations needed powerful tools they could trust and control completely.
Early 2024
Building the Foundation
We assembled a team of distributed systems experts and security engineers to build a reactive architecture capable of processing data at unprecedented scale. The goal: match cloud vendor performance while maintaining complete data sovereignty.
Mid 2024
Breakthrough Performance
Our C++ regex engine achieved 5-10x performance improvements over traditional solutions. Combined with GPU acceleration readiness, Data Hawk became the fastest self-hosted data governance platform available.
Today
Empowering Enterprises
Data Hawk now protects sensitive data for organizations worldwide, processing petabytes daily while maintaining sub-100ms latency. Our commitment to self-hosted excellence continues to drive innovation.
